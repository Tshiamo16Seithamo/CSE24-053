Our Story

Born in Botswana, Worn Worldwide

Founded in 2023, Gaff is a premium t-shirt brand that combines African-inspired designs with contemporary streetwear aesthetics. Each piece is crafted with the highest quality materials to ensure comfort and durability.

Our Mission

To redefine African streetwear by creating timeless pieces that celebrate our heritage while appealing to global fashion trends.
